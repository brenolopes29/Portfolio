HOLA! I'm Breno, born in Taubaté, in the interior of São Paulo, Brazil. Since I was young, I've always loved video games. My first console was a Mega Drive, which my father bought (or received?) from my uncle. It was a Japanese Mega Drive, the fat version. I remember it was a struggle to connect it to the TV since our TV didn't have an RCA input. I ended up playing it only a few times because the adapter kept breaking. It came with three games, but I don’t remember exactly which ones. One was about a knight in iron armor who, when taking damage, would turn into an old man with a white beard. Another one took place in the jungle, but I don’t recall much about it. The third cartridge didn’t even work. After that, I had several consoles: Polystation, PS2, PC, Game Boy, Xbox 360, PS4, and PS5. But what I played the most was on the computer (damn League of Legends). It was love at first sight. I've always enjoyed tinkering, or "messing around," as my dad would say. I thought I was a genius, editing and making slideshow presentations with family photos, with generic Windows music playing in the background. I even tried to create an operating system with a friend in 2012… obviously, it didn’t work out. I always wanted to be part of this world. When I was 11, I was already thinking about studying Computer Science. As I grew up, I took different paths, but I was always close to computers, working on Warcraft 3 servers and, in 2012, swapping PC parts and doing other things. Since 2022, many things have changed. I changed jobs, and since 2024, I've been seriously considering getting into the IT field. In 2025, I decided to enroll in ADS (Analysis and Systems Development) at Uninter, with an expected graduation in the second quarter of 2027.
